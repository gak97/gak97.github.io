About me

I am a PhD student at the University of Surrey in the UK working on A Multimodal Framework for Hate Speech Detection on Social Media Platforms under the supervision of Dr. Diptesh Kanojia and Prof. Helen Treharne.

I received a Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence from the University of Strathclyde, UK in 2023. I also have a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the RV College of Engineering in Bengaluru, India.

I became a chess Grandmaster in 2019 and an International Master in 2013. I have won several national and international chess tournaments. For more information, see my Chess page.
